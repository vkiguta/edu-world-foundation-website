Testimonials from our members

I have learnt so much about myself in the process of offering support to others and it really has changed my life. Every day as a part of EWF brings something new, and I feel very lucky and extremely fulfilled to be part of such an experience: I will certainly continue to be a part of it wherever life takes me.

Nancy Mwaniki (director)

Upon starting my volunteering, I was initially really nervous. Because I didn’t have any professional background in education, I was really worried that I wouldn’t say the right things and that I didn’t have enough knowledge to help someone change their situation. The great thing about EWF was how supportive the team is -I knew I could ask for help with absolutely anything.

Victor Kiguta(volunteer)

Through EWF's Youth Apprenticeship Program, I have been able to not only develop skills but be more mentally aware of what life holds.

Melvin Mwangi (graduate)
